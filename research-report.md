# Research Report

This site is built from official Super Rugby Pacific sources checked on June 5, 2026. The official tournament format page confirms that Super Rugby Pacific 2026 runs across an 11-team competition with an 83-match season over 16 rounds, followed by a three-week finals series. The same page confirms the 2026 finals structure: qualifying finals for 1 v 6, 2 v 5, and 3 v 4; three winners plus the highest-ranked losing team advance to the semi-finals; and the final is hosted by the top-ranked semi-final winner. The official qualifying-finals coverage confirms the week-one fixtures and the key 2026 twist that the highest-ranked losing team progresses as the fourth seed and therefore cannot host a semi-final or final. The official June 5 match report confirms that the Hurricanes beat the ACT Brumbies 66-12 in Wellington to become the first side through to the semi-finals. At the time of writing, the Crusaders v Blues and Chiefs v Queensland Reds qualifiers remain the next live bracket points to watch.
